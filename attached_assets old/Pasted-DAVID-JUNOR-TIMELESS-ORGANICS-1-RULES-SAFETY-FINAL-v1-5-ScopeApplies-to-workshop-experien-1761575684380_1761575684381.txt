1) RULES & SAFETY (FINAL v1.5)

ScopeApplies to workshop experiences (including Founders 100 sessions).

Age

Purchases: 18+ (if under 18, a guardian must purchase in their own name).

16–17: “Assist, no pour.” Allowed: non‑hazardous prep (cleaning, spruing, investing — under supervision, basic finishing). All pouring/casting is done by us.

Under 16: Observe only. No tools.

Molten‑metal & furnace work: 18+ only. No exceptions.

PPE & ClothingClosed robust shoes, long pants, natural‑fibre tops. Tie back hair; avoid loose sleeves/jewellery. Studio PPE must be worn as instructed.

Conduct & ZonesStay in marked safe zones unless invited forward. Follow instructions the first time. No alcohol/substances during the session. Safety breaches may end participation with no refund.

Specimens (what you can cast)

Organic specimens only (no shells/pressurised items/batteries/resin). We may refuse unsafe/unsuitable items.

Choose from our on‑site studio specimens or bring your own and decide when you arrive.

Seasonal availability: not all plants/flowers are year‑round; every season makes beautiful work.

MediaWe may film/photograph sessions. Opt‑out available (see Waiver/Consent). Minors require guardian media release.

HealthTell us about respiratory issues, pacemakers, allergies, or injuries in advance. Heat, dust, and noise are present.

Property & TurnaroundWe aren’t responsible for lost personal items. Finishing/patina typically ~5 business days (shipping optional).

Medical ResponseWe maintain a CMR emergency response membership for on‑site response/stabilisation during sessions. This is not a substitute for personal medical aid/insurance; hospital transport/care may incur costs.

Post‑Session & FeedbackAfter hot‑work is complete, relax in the safe zone. We welcome feedback — a short feedback form is provided on‑site. With explicit consent, we may quote your name and country with your feedback on our website/social channels; otherwise we’ll use it anonymously.

4) FAQ (FINAL v1.2)

Q: Is there an age restriction?A: Purchases are 18+. Ages 16–17 may assist with non‑hazardous prep (cleaning, spruing, investing under supervision, basic finishing). No pouring/molten metal for under‑18s. Under 16 may observe only.

Q: Do 16–17s need a guardian present?A: Guardian Consent & Indemnity is required. Guardian must be contactable during the session.

Q: What should I wear?A: Closed robust shoes, long pants, natural‑fibre tops. Tie back hair; avoid loose sleeves/jewellery. PPE supplied on site.

Q: Is alcohol allowed?A: No alcohol or substances before or during the session—safety first. After we’re done, you’re welcome to relax in the safe zone. We welcome post‑session feedback — it helps improve the experience. (If you plan to drink, please arrange safe transport.)

Q: What can I bring to cast?A: Organic specimens only (no shells/pressurised items/batteries/resin). We may refuse unsafe/unsuitable items. You can choose from our range of studio specimens on site or bring your own and decide when you arrive. Availability is seasonal — not all plants/flowers are year‑round, and every season makes beautiful work.

Q: Who does the pour?A: Our team handles all molten‑metal operations. Guests may assist with approved non‑hazardous prep only (16–17 with consent).

Q: How long is the experience?A: Expect 1.5–2.5 hours depending on the piece and group size.

Q: When do I get my finished piece?A: Standard finishing and patina take ~5 business days. We’ll notify you for collection or arrange shipping.

Q: Do you ship? International?A: Yes—SA and international. Shipping quoted at checkout or after weighing. Customs/VAT at destination are the recipient’s responsibility.

Q: Can you publish my feedback?A: Yes — opt in on the form. With consent we may quote your name + country; otherwise we’ll publish anonymously. (POPIA‑compliant; consent can be withdrawn.)

Q: Contact?A: studio@timeless.organic. For any urgent issues, the confirmation email includes a mobile number.
